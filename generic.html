Motivation

Our motivation for this project was that in our group, everyone has some sort of interest in being fit and healthy.There are a lot of fitness applications out there but that helps people to be fit but, will cost the user money. Whereas, our app it is free of cost and has everything that a user could look for in an app from personalised fitness plans, meal and workout suggestions, etc.

This project is an important one, as obesity is a severe problem consuming around 30% of the worldâ€™s population, and is expected to increase over the course of the next few decades due to lifestyle changes and convenient technologies, such as computers and TVs. This is also because many people are not conscious of their health and among those who are conscious, do not know what path to follow. According to a survey, 58% of smartphone users have downloaded a fitness app. And among these, about 52 percent of users said they downloaded health apps to track physical activity, while 47 percent wanted to track what they ate. Forty-six percent said they downloaded health apps to lose weight and 34 percent said they wanted to use health apps to learn exercises. For the future employer, it would show that even the app is free of cost the employer could also make money out of the app.
